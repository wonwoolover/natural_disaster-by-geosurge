Precautions:
What to Prepare
in Emergency Cases

Precautions are the proactive steps we take to reduce the impact of natural disasters and protect ourselves. This includes preparing an emergency kit, creating a family evacuation plan and staying updated with alerts. Being prepared not only saves lives but also helps communities recover faster.

Natural Disasters -
What We Should All Know

Natural disasters such as earthquakes, floods, hurricanes, wildfires, and tsunamis can strike without warning, causing widespread damage and endangering lives. Understanding the nature of these events and being prepared is essential for ensuring personal safety and minimizing risk.

Landslides in Myanmar are becoming increasingly common due to deforestation. Steep slopes make mountainous areas particularly vulnerable to deadly landslides that can bury entire communities.

Our Purpose

The purpose of making this website
Education and Awareness:
